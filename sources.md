## Agent commissions
- **Federal Reserve Bank of Richmond** — Study calling out the US commission system as a global anomaly; cites <2% norm in UK, Ireland, Netherlands, Singapore, Sweden, Norway; notes buyers commonly unrepresented in Australia, Canada, Denmark.
- **LinkedIn industry posts** — Country-by-country breakdown of commission percentages.
- **Property118** — India 1.7%, Hong Kong 0.75%, South Korea 0.55%, South Africa 7.5%.
- **Allrealestateislocal** — Japan legal cap 3% + 60,000 JPY.
- **Mortgagecalculator** — US agents collect ~$75 billion/year in commissions.

## Mortgage interest math
- **Mortgage Calculator** — $200k @ 4% / 30yr → $143,739 interest; payment #153 crossover.
- **Bankrate** — 2026 US average 30-yr rate 6.63%; $500k @ 6.63% / 30yr ≈ $651k interest; warning that resetting the amortization clock by refinancing pushes you back to peak-interest months.
